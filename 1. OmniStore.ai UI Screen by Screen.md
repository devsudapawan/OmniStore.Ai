# 1. OmniStore.Ai UI Screen by Screen

This document details every screen, its layout, and the explicit UI elements according to the Thumb-First and High-Readability design systems.

## 🎯 1. First-Time Setup & Onboarding (Zero-Setup Flow)

**Screen 1 — Splash Screen**
- **Elements:** App logo centered. Dark background (`#0d1117` in dark mode, `#1a1f2e` in light mode). 
- **Tagline:** “Run your shop. Even offline.”

**Screen 2 — Language Selection**
- **Elements:** Large, tap-friendly buttons for available languages (English, Hindi, etc.).
- **Behavior:** Selection is stored locally and applied universally across the app.

**Screen 3 — Business Setup & Questionnaire**
- **Elements:** 5 to 7 simple questions presented linearly or in a wizard.
  - Business type (Grocery, Dairy, Medical, etc.)
  - Products, Services, or Both?
  - Do you sell on credit?
  - Accepted payment methods (Cash, UPI, etc.)
  - Primary stock unit (kg / pcs / liters)
  - Reminder preference
  - Currency format
- **Rules:** Big text, minimal clutter. One primary button `Continue` at the bottom.

**Screen 4 — Auto-Setup Loader**
- **Elements:** Simple loading animation or Shimmer effect.
- **Text:** “Setting up your business…”
- **Behind the scenes:** System generates default categories, payment methods, credit rules, and inventory templates.

## 🏠 2. Home Dashboard

The first screen the user sees every day.

**Layout Structure (Top to Bottom):**
- **App Bar (Top):**
  - **Height:** `56dp`
  - **Left:** Title or Shop Name.
  - **Right:** Quick action icons (Settings, Sync status).
- **Section 1 — Summary Cards (Horizontal Scroll):**
  - **Height & Spacing:** `12dp` between cards. Same padding everywhere (`16dp`).
  - **Cards:** `Today’s sales`, `Pending credit`, `Low stock`.
- **Section 2 — Quick Actions:**
  - **Grid Layout:** 2 per row.
  - **Buttons:** `Add Sale`, `Add Payment`, `Add Product`.
- **Section 3 — Recent Activity:**
  - Scrollable list of the last few transactions.
- **Floating Action Button (FAB):**
  - **Position:** Bottom right.
  - **Action:** ➕ Add Transaction.

---

## ➕ 3. Add Transaction Screen (Most Critical)

This screen must be accessible and fast to complete.

**Layout Structure:**
- **Customer Selector:** Quick search or selection of a returning customer, or "Walk-in".
- **Product List:**
  - Fast selection of products being sold.
  - Includes quantity adjustment `+/-`.
- **Total Section:** Summarizes the items and shows the grand total clearly.
- **Payment Type Selector:** Cash, Credit (Udhar), UPI.
- **Sticky Save Button:**
  - **Position:** Anchored to bottom.
  - **Style:** Primary full-width button. Height: `48-52dp`.

---

## 👥 4. Customers Screen

Manages all customers and their credit ledgers.

**Layout Structure:**
- **Search Bar:** Top pinned for fast lookups.
- **Customer Cards List:**
  - Each card includes: Name, Balance (Green for neutral/positive, Red for owed), Last activity date.
- **FAB:** Add new customer.

**4.1 Customer Detail View:**
- **Top Info:** Profile details, Current calculated balance, Trust indicator.
- **Action Buttons:** Send reminder (WhatsApp/SMS).
- **History List:** Full transaction history (Sales and Payments).

---

## 📦 5. Inventory Screen

Manages product stock logically.

**Layout Structure:**
- **Category Chips:** Horizontal scrolling chips at the top to filter items (e.g., Dairy, Snacks).
- **Product List:**
  - Each list item shows: Product Name, Price, and Current Stock (calculated).
  - Highlights low-stock items with a Yellow warning color.
- **FAB:** Add new product or stock adjustment.

---

## 🤖 6. AI Assistant Screen

Chat-style interface optimized for transaction conversion.

**Layout Structure:**
- **Conversation Window:** Displays the user's queries and AI responses.
- **Suggestions Strip:** Quick chips for common commands (e.g., "Add sale", "Who owes money?").
- **Bottom Input:**
  - Text input field.
  - Large Voice Input (Microphone) button.

---

## 📱 7. Global Navigation Components

**Bottom Navigation Bar (Max 5 Tabs):**
1. Home 🏠
2. Customers 👥
3. Inventory 📦
4. Records 🧾
5. Assistant 🤖

**Card Design Rules (Used uniformly):**
- Thumb-first design context.
- Inside Padding: `12dp`.
- Margins between Cards: `12dp` or `16dp`.
- High contrast typography for primary numbers and data points. Only 4 distinct semantic colors are used (Green, Red, Yellow, Blue).
